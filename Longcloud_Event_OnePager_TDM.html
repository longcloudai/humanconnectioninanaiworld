The Q&A
Paul Singh × Trent Montgomery
TDM Transport · Waikato, NZ
Less admin, more road
Paul spoke with TDM's Managing Director Trent Montgomery about the real-time platform Longcloud built for the Waikato logistics leader: an office portal and a mobile driver app running on one shared database. Dispatch, jobs, CRM, fleet, maintenance and reporting now live in one place, while drivers see their runs, snap photo proof of pickup and delivery, and message the office as jobs happen. Vehicle compliance is tracked automatically, so nothing slips through the cracks. Rather than waiting for the pains of scale to bite, Trent invested early, laying the foundation for AI-assisted scheduling, route optimisation and smarter operations as TDM keeps growing.

The Talk
Nick Foley · Longcloud
Small country, big leverage
Nick takes a look at the New Zealand video game industry: the studios, the talent and the stories being built right here, and what it tells us about creativity, community and human connection in a world increasingly shaped by AI. He digs into the missed opportunities too, and how, by harnessing collaboration and our unique culture, we can establish ourselves as a world leader in the industry. Oh, and Nick even had our AV guy, Rob, play a game live in front of the audience, one Nick had built himself ahead of the event, for everyone's entertainment. You had to be there!
